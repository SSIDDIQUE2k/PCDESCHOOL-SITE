School-Cred

This is our school, where peace dwells. Let the rooms be filled with contentment. Love abides here: love of one another, love of mankind, love of life itself, and love of God. Let us remember that, just as many hands build a house, so many hearts make a school.

Belief Statement

We believe:

  • Learning is a lifelong journey.
  • Children, families, and the school community share responsibility for learning and behavior.
  • Everyone thrives in a safe environment.
  • Each staff member provides an atmosphere where children can question and explore, which helps them develop skills to solve problems independently and collaboratively.
  • Respect for others must be modeled, so children can experience what kindness looks like and feels like as they interact with others.
  • All students can learn, but not all learn in the same way or at the same pace.
  • Regular attendance, punctuality, and preparedness are essential for success in school.
  • Change is a source of growth and development.
  • We can accomplish more by working together than we can by working alone.
