Morning Arrival Procedures

Students being dropped off should not be sent up to classrooms unescorted. Students must arrive no later than 8:30 A.M. Arriving after 8:30 A.M is considered tardiness, and they may be sent home. Doors will be locked at 8:45 A.M.

Please make every effort to ensure that your child arrives at school by 8:30 A.M. Arriving late negatively impacts the learning of your child, as well as interrupts the learning of others.

Dismissal Procedures

  • All students, with the exception of those in after-school or tutoring programs, will be dismissed at 3:30 P.M. Day Care (Pre-Nursery - Kindergarten) until 6:00 P.M.
  • If someone other than a parent or guardian is picking up your child, notification must be given in writing.
  • It is assumed most students will ride the school bus. If your child is not going to ride the bus, or if they will be riding a different bus at any time, a note must be sent to the school office notifying us before we will allow your child to make a change. Please send these notes in with your child.
  • Parents are requested to pick up their children at 3:30 P.M. There is a 15 minute grace period.
    • A late fee of $10.00 will be charged for every 15 minutes after 3:45 P.M. for elementary and middle school students. For Pre-Nursery, Kindergarten, and after-school students, a late fee of $10.00 will be charged for every 15 minutes after 6:00 P.M. (NO EXCEPTIONS).
