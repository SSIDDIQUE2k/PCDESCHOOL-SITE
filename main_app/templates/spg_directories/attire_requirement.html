Dress Code

Students must adhere to the following dress code guidelines:

  • Students must be fully attired in school uniform. Uniforms should be worn during early Fall and Spring.
  • Fourth grade and above are mandated to wear Oxford shirts and navy blue shoes.
  • Girls wear a khaki vest, and both boys and girls wear blazers.
  • Any changes outside of the normal dress code will be noted on the monthly calendar.
  • Colorful sneakers and black shoes are not allowed.

In the event clothing is deemed inappropriate:

  • You will be phoned to bring a change of clothing, or your child will be sent home.
  • Students should dress for the weather conditions. In winter, boots, hats, coats, gloves, or mittens are necessary.
  • Students should walk with their uniform, including shoes, to change once they enter the building.

Additional dress code rules:

  • For girls, stud and/or small hoop earrings are allowed.
  • Boys are prohibited from wearing earrings and braided hair.
  • When uniforms are not required, shorts of appropriate length can be worn.
  • Please label all clothing to avoid confusion and loss.

Physical Education (Gym)

Specific guidelines for gym attire will be provided separately to ensure appropriate dress for physical activities.
