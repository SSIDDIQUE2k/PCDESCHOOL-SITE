Student Conduct and Discipline

A Code of Discipline is provided by the Julia Nicholson Leadership Academy. Please become familiar with the expectations and consequences. Areas addressed include bus, playground, classroom, hallway, bathroom, after-school behavior, academic dishonesty, language, vandalism, fighting, disrespect to peers and staff members, and harassment.

Students and staff at Julia Nicholson Leadership Academy work together to construct a community that is orderly, safe, respectful, and friendly. We believe that all children have the right to learn and play in a school that is free from harassment.

Misbehavior may result in the following consequences:

  • Inappropriate behavior may warrant removal from the classroom.
  • Students may be sent to the principal's office to discuss their behavior and the consequences.
  • Continued inappropriate behavior may warrant parents being called in.
  • Based on the severity of the violation, suspension may be warranted.
  • Severe misconduct may lead to expulsion at the discretion of the administration.
