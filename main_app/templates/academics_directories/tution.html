Tuition and School Fees

Tuition/School Fees for Pre-Nursery through Eighth Grade is due on the first of each month. A grace period is extended until the fifth. After the fifth, a late fee of $25 will be applied to your account.

If tuition is not paid by the tenth of the month, the student will not be admitted to the school until the tuition is paid in full. Should a student enter the building, they will be retained in the office and the parents will be immediately notified.

All fees are non-refundable.
